About Me

I am a dedicated cybersecurity enthusiast with a deep passion for securing cloud environments and empowering the next generation of African women in tech. My unique drive stems from my commitment to mastering AWS security while fostering a community where young African ladies can thrive in technology.

My journey in cybersecurity is fueled by hands-on experience in securing AWS infrastructures, focusing on threat detection, identity management, and compliance. Through continuous learning, certifications, and real-world application, I strive to become an AWS Security Architect, ensuring robust cloud security for organizations worldwide.

Beyond my personal growth, I am committed to mentorship and advocacy, inspiring young African women to break barriers in tech. By engaging in cybersecurity forums, sharing insights, and participating in industry discussions, I aim to create opportunities for aspiring female tech professionals to excel in cloud security.

My job in AWS cybersecurity enables me to live out this passion by providing the expertise and platform needed to contribute to a more secure digital world while uplifting and guiding the next generation of African women in technology.
